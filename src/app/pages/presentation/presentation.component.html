Speaking strictly, compendium of practical chess knowledge and entertainment.

It's innovative tool designed both for beginners who want to start their chess adventure and intermediate players interested in improving their skills.

As we all know, practice makes perfect. So, what kind of training do we offer?
First of all, you can try to face, more or less difficult, abilities challenges in the form of engaging chess puzzles like checkmates, stalemates or double attacks.

Besides specific moves, you'll learn the most important in-game principles, fundamental strategies and clever maneuvers.

On our website you can also find plenty of thematic articles about whole game history, best professional players and many curiosities that may help you broaden your chess horizons.

Last but not least, Chess Training Game can boast of fantastic users community gathered in discussion forum. Exciting discourses and exchanges of views await every player, doesn't matter if greenhorn or expert.
